Welcome to Le Méridien

A Gateway to Discovery in Oran, Algeria

Perched on a cliff revealing breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea, Le Méridien Oran Hotel & Convention Centre is a call to discovery. Our 5-star property blends sophisticated design with warm Algerian hospitality, creating an unforgettable retreat for leisure and business travelers alike.

Featuring balconies with floor-to-ceiling windows, refined restaurants, a rejuvenating spa, and a state-of-the-art convention center — every moment here is crafted for excellence.
